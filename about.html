About Blunova Properties

Our Story

Blunova Properties was founded in 2010 with a vision to revolutionize the luxury rental market in Tennessee. Our founders, Sarah Thompson and Michael Chen, saw an opportunity to blend modern design with Southern charm, creating unique living spaces that cater to the discerning renter.

Starting with a single property in Nashville, we've grown to manage over 50 premium rentals across Tennessee's major cities. Our success is built on our commitment to quality, attention to detail, and dedication to providing an unparalleled living experience for our tenants.

Our Mission

At Blunova Properties, our mission is to redefine luxury living in Tennessee. We strive to create homes that inspire, comfort, and delight our residents. By carefully curating our properties and providing exceptional service, we aim to exceed expectations and set new standards in the rental market.

Our Values

  • Quality: We never compromise on the quality of our properties or services.
  • Innovation: We continuously seek new ways to improve and enhance the living experience.
  • Integrity: We operate with honesty and transparency in all our dealings.
  • Community: We believe in fostering a sense of belonging among our residents and in the neighborhoods we serve.
  • Sustainability: We are committed to implementing eco-friendly practices in our properties and operations.
